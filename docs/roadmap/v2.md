# v2.0.0 — Habits & Analytics (unscheduled)

Sourced directly from `docs/product/scope.md` → "v2 — Habits & Analytics". This file is intentionally **not** broken into sprints or days yet.

## Why this stays unscheduled

Planning v2 day-by-day before v1.0.0 ships and has been used for at least a week is premature — you don't yet have real usage data to know which of these matters most (per the Product Success Metrics in `docs/product/goals.md`: % of scheduled reviews completed, retention, sync uptime). Scheduling this now would be planning in the dark.

## Scope (high-level only)

- Automatic +1 upsolving engine (full version, beyond the v1 BR-007 baseline)
- Pattern weakness heatmaps
- Weekly email summaries
- Discord and Telegram notifications
- Custom learning goals
- Contest reminders
- Advanced dashboards

## Entry criteria for planning v2 in detail

Do not turn this into a sprint plan until:
1. `v1.0.0` is tagged and deployed to production
2. You've personally used it for at least 7 consecutive days
3. You've identified — from real use, not speculation — which v2 item you'd actually miss if it didn't exist

At that point, mirror the structure of `v1.md`: day-by-day sprints with FR/US codes. Not before.
