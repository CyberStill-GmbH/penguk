# Vision - Penguk

## Problem Statement

Competitive programming progress is fragmented across different platforms and tools. This fragmentation makes it difficult to track your overall progress, evaluate if your study methods are working, or provide a clear guide for others to follow.

## Solution

Penguk proposes a way to take ownership of your progress using structured study methods like spaced repetition, reminders, and frustration management.

Key features include:

- **Centralized Progress Hub:** A single dashboard to view your topics and programming languages across different platforms.
- **Connected Notes System:** Link your progress to your favorite notes app or Git repository.
- **Contest Reminders & "+1" Upsolves:** Stay on top of contests and enforce discipline with obligatory "+1" upsolving (solving the next level of difficulty).

## Value Proposition

- **Personalized Progress:** View your growth, set personal goals, and use a spaced repetition method that fits your preferences.
- **Organized Workflow:** Access your notes, upsolves, and contest history in one clean space, without dealing with scattered local files.

## Target Users

- Competitive programmers.
- Technical job seekers.
- Anyone wanting to improve and track their logical programming skills.

## Product Vision

An open-source platform that helps people overcome the frustration of hitting plateaus, guides them toward the study methods that work best for them, and serves as a clear reference for everyone navigating competitive programming (CP).
